About Me

Born and raised in Vancouver, I thrive on challenges and seek opportunities to expand my horizons, whether it's through academic endeavors or an active lifestyle that includes sports like soccer and volleyball. With a strong foundation in engineering principles, honed communication skills from teamwork, and a mindset of constant improvement, I'm poised to tackle complex problems and drive technological advancements.
