About Me

Hello! I'm a driven individual, born and raised in Vancouver. Currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Applied Science with a specialization in Mechanical Engineering at the University of British Columbia. My passion lies in exploring the intersection of engineering and innovation.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I embrace an active lifestyle. You can often find me at the gym, engaging in soccer or volleyball matches, or immersing myself in the latest developments in the world of technology, cars, and gadgets. Through various experiences, including group projects, jobs, and sports teams, I've honed my communication, leadership, and teamwork skills.

I thrive on challenges and continuously seek opportunities to broaden my horizons and expand my knowledge. Stepping outside of my comfort zone is a way for me to experiment, grow, and discover new possibilities.

One of my core values is relentless improvement. Even when I achieve exceptional results, I always ask myself, "Is there more I can do?" This mindset drives me to actively pursue opportunities that foster personal and professional growth. It has been instrumental in my past successes and will continue to propel me forward.

Currently, I'm an active member of the UBC Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS) team, contributing to the payload subteam. Collaborating with fellow members, I design and prototype mechanical gizmos essential for competitions. Our current focus is on developing a cutting-edge concept airbus for the AEAC UAS competition, and we are dedicated to becoming the best team in the field.

Throughout my academic journey, I've consistently excelled, maintaining a stellar 3.95 GPA. Recognition in the form of scholarships such as the BGC Canada Scholarship and Best Buy Student Scholarship further validates my commitment to excellence.

I'm eager to leverage my skills, passion for innovation, and dedication to continuous improvement to contribute meaningfully to future endeavors. Let's connect and explore new possibilities together!
